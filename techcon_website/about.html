Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate developers and has since grown into one of the world’s most influential technology conferences. Over the years, it has provided a platform for groundbreaking ideas, industry-shaping announcements, and global networking.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and create opportunities for everyone in the tech ecosystem. We believe in pushing the boundaries of what’s possible and driving positive change through technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Recognized as one of the first computer programmers, Ada Lovelace’s pioneering work continues to inspire generations of innovators.

Alan Turing

Mathematician and codebreaker, Alan Turing’s contributions to computing laid the foundation for modern computer science.

Grace Hopper

A trailblazing computer scientist and naval officer, Grace Hopper was instrumental in developing early programming languages and concepts.
